# Location: Carnival

## Rumors
- Flyers with devil imagery were posted to draw attention to the carnival.
- Some showed two giant horns and the name Schi, while others looked like ordinary carnival flyers.
- Strange prizes, rituals, and ring-hunting were tied to the carnival.
- Battle of the bands took place there.

## What is Known
- Carnival was a major gathering place and event.
- Flyers were used to spread word of it.
- RingMaster Urie oversaw the main tent.
- There was also a psychic associated with the carnival.
- The carnival became tied to the search for twelve rings.
- Jax taught a ritual that could be used to find rings.
- One ring was in the psychic’s tent.
- Battle of the bands took place there, with Panic at the Pub among the participants.
- The place also led the party into other connected adventures beyond the carnival grounds.

## History
- During the events later recorded in the 2021-10-28 session recap, the carnival became the center of the twelve-rings thread.
- Characters were drawn into rings, rituals, visions, strange prizes, and performances.
- The carnival also served as the starting point for later travel to the Marsh of Sorrows and the BooNazEE / Madam Gravor conflict.

# Location: Cathrys

## Rumors
- Cathrys is a jungle prison-world where even the grass wants blood.
- Hidden acid pits lie beneath patches of harmless-looking growth.
- There are pockets within Cathrys that do not belong, bright places that feel divine rather than damned.

## What is Known
- Cathrys is a layer of Carceri made of red jungle growth, razor grass, acid-saturated ground, and dangerous hidden pits.
- The worlds of Carceri are described as stringed like pearls, and Cathrys lies along that descent.
- Known features of the region encountered there include:
  - Razor-blade grass
  - Acid-saturated ground
  - Red jungle growth
  - Pools or bogs capable of hiding deadly creatures
  - A blackstone dock reached from the River Styx
  - A red cave with colossal iron doors
  - Abi's hut, built from razor grass with matching furniture
  - A path leading toward [[The Steading]]
- A mezzoloth named [[Abi]] was encountered there and offered guidance deeper into the plane.
- Abi had not seen Loki or Idunn, despite the party seeking them.
- Abi is immune to the acid pits and cutting foliage of Cathrys, allowing her to guide others through it.
- The first major encounter in Cathrys involved black puddings rising from acid pools.
- The terrain itself functioned as an active hazard because of acid and razor grass.
- The party also encountered the Arch of Justice within Cathrys, where each traveler had to confess a deepest truth in order to pass onward.
- Abi described [[Minethys]] as the next lower layer, a cold and sandblasted wasteland.

## History
- At the Arch of Justice, each member of the party revealed a hidden truth before being allowed onward into Cathrys.
- Upon arriving deeper within the plane, they immediately discovered its hostile terrain and were attacked by black puddings emerging from the acid pools.
- Abi later informed them that they were in Cathrys and that escape from the plane was nearly impossible.
- The party later followed Abi through Cathrys to her razor-grass hut, where she spoke of her shame and reluctance to approach [[The Steading]].
- From there, Abi guided the party safely through hidden acid pits until the red jungle gave way to the green fields of The Steading.

# Abi

- She is a mezzoloth encountered in Carceri.
- She first warned the party about the danger beneath their feet after the black pudding fight.
- She explained that the party was in Carceri, also called Tartarus.
- She told the party that leaving Carceri was nearly impossible.
- She had not seen Loki or Iðunn.
- She offered to guide the party deeper into the plane.
- She is immune to the acid pits and cutting foliage of Cathrys.
- She led the party through razor grass, hidden acid pits, and the red jungle toward [[The Steading]].
- She lives in a hut made from razor grass, with furniture made of the same deadly material.
- She carries deep shame over having eaten her nephews after a goblin sapper attack destroyed her village and she believed they were the only survivors.
- She later learned another village had been only a few miles away.
- She feels she deserves Carceri and was reluctant to enter The Steading.
- She speaks with a weary, matter-of-fact manner.

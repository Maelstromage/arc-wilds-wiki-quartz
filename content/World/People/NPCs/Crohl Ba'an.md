# Crohl Ba'an

- Two-headed ogre wizard or oni
- Not usually present at the Swamp Hut
- Tied to power placed in him by an Old God
- Connected to the threat of one thousand years of darkness
- The Old God wants Crohl Ba'an dead so that the power in him can return
- If that power returns, the Old God may be released into the world
- Last known to be in the Cloven Hoof Cave

# Marmalade

- Small bell-shaped red slime
- Described by [[Xavriel]] as a rogue gelatinous blood clot
- Found in a small side chamber within [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Initially frightened and hid in a hole in the wall
- Responded to gentler treatment and to Umbra singing a lullaby
- Whistled back, emerged willingly, and climbed onto Umbra's shoulder
- Was given a tiny sweater by the party
- The party chose to keep Marmalade rather than deliver him to Xavriel
- A memorable living remnant of the temple's strange blood-based magic
- rides around in a glass jar with little handles on each side, wears it like pants.

# Philosopher's Stones

- Rare transmuted stones created from pure life substance within [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Associated with [[Xavriel]] and his experiments in the ley-line chamber
- Created by refining concentrated life substance drawn through the temple's deeper systems
- Not all philosopher's stones are identical; different stones may produce different effects
- Known effects include:
  - healing wounds
  - offering protective magical benefits
  - transmuting objects or flesh into gold
  - promises or theories of immortality
- At least one unstable stone turned a creature's hand into solid gold on contact
- At least some stones appear unstable, experimental, or dangerous to handle directly
- Philosopher's stones appear to contain limited magical power rather than endless use
- Their creation is dangerous, poorly understood, and tied to deeper magical forces within the ruins
- Xavriel's work suggests the stones are not relics merely found in the ruins, but products he is actively attempting to manufacture

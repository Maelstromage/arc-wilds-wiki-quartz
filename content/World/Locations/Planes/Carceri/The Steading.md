# Location: The Steading

## Rumors
- There is a patch of blue sky and green pasture hidden impossibly inside Carceri.
- A giantess there welcomes little folk, so long as they are not stealing from her pantry.
- Something is living in the walls beneath the feast table.

## What is Known
- The Steading is a divine pocket realm within Cathrys in Carceri.
- It is tied to [[Grolantor]], a giant god of food.
- The realm stands in sharp contrast to the prison plane around it, with blue skies, green grass, and giant-sized crops.
- [[Martha]] lives there in a massive house scaled for a giant around 150 feet tall.
- Martha was preparing a feast for a festival honoring Grolantor when the party arrived.
- Martha had previously relied on tiny helpers who were killed by her husband after he blamed them for bringing rats into the house.
- The party was asked to deal with pests raiding Martha's food stores.
- Those pests turned out to include giant scorpions on the table and at least one giant rat inside the wall passages.
- A mouse-hole opening in Martha's house contains the remnants of a tiny living space with matchstick furniture, thread hammocks, and hidden contraptions.
- Within that hidden space the party found a small biplane, immovable rods, and a wand of wonder.
- [[Jesse]] is expected to arrive there and is said to know a trickster god on the next layer.

## History
- The party reached The Steading after following [[Abi]] through the deadly terrain of Cathrys.
- Abi hesitated to enter the realm because of shame over her past, but still guided the party to its edge.
- Martha welcomed the party and brought them into her giant home.
- While helping Martha, the party fought giant scorpions across her dining table and later killed a giant rat in the wall refuge.
- After Martha's husband attacked the party in confusion, she drove him off and later confirmed he would be away for the night.
- The party fortified the mouse-hole refuge and rested there while waiting for Jesse.

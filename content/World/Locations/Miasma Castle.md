# Location: Miasma Castle

## Rumors
- Miasma Castle is a cursed stronghold wrapped in unnatural miasma.
- Those who die within the miasma rise again as undead.
- It is said that leaving the miasma can make a person deathly sick.
- Rumor claims the king within possesses a vorpal sword.
- The castle’s fall is tied to a beholder, a betrayal, and dark bargains.

## What is Known
- Miasma Castle is a cursed castle swallowed by miasma and rot.
- The miasma is central to the castle’s corruption.
- The dead within its influence may rise as undead.
- The castle is tied to King Cyril, Princess Cecilia, Ogmarth, Stewart, and Brottor Burrowfound.
- A beholder is bound up in the story of the castle’s downfall.
- The land around the castle became a swamp after the curse took hold.
- The place still holds royal chambers, barracks, jail cells, hidden rooms, and treasures from an older kingdom.

## History
- Miasma Castle was once a royal stronghold under King Cyril.
- Brottor Burrowfound was helping the king as an engineer and was involved in a mission concerning a lodestone for a flying ship.
- Stewart, the majordomo, brought a beholder into the kingdom.
- To deal with the beholder, Stewart made a bargain with hags.
- Instead of solving the problem, that bargain led to the miasma and the transformation of the surrounding land into a swamp.
- The castle is now a place of undead, broken loyalties, and unfinished royal business.

## People
### Ogmarth

### Princess Cecilia

### King Cyril
- Former ruler of the castle

### Stewart
- Majordomo whose actions helped doom the castle

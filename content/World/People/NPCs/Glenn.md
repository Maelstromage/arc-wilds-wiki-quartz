# Glenn

- Legendary dwarven master builder encountered in Mount Celestia
- Enormous, red-bearded, bright-eyed dwarf of great skill and confidence
- Oversees astonishing celestial construction projects
- Agreed to design and build a fortress for the party in the material plane
- Named his price as one golden apple belonging to the goddess [[Idunn]]
- Travels by a flying boat drawn by gigantic goats
- Likely one of the finest builders the party has ever encountered

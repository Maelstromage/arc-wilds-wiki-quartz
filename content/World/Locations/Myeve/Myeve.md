# Location: Myeve

# 1. Rumors
- “There’s no walls, but nothing attacks the town.”
- “People say you’re safe for miles out.”
- “Adventurers come here to rest. When they leave the safety of Myeve, some never return.”
- “No one knows why it’s safe, and no one asks too many questions.”
- “If the Arc Wilds has a last lantern before the dark, it’s Myeve.”

# 2. What is Known
- Myeve is a small frontier settlement in the Arc Wilds.
- It is approached by sea through thick fog, with high cliffs flanking the narrow approach.
- A dark marsh lies to the west of the settlement.
- It functions as a central safe hub for adventurers, merchants, and local folk trying to survive and profit on the edge of dangerous country.
- The settlement has no walls and no obvious visible defenses.
- Its layout appears organic, with dirt roads and some cobblestone paths added over time rather than according to any formal plan.
- Despite its lack of visible fortification, Myeve is treated as functionally safe.
- Large-scale threats do not seem to enter or attack the town.
- That protection is real, but not openly explained.
- The safety of Myeve does not extend very far into the surrounding wilds, where danger returns quickly.
- Important known locations in town include:
  - **Odin’s Eye**, the main tavern and social hub
  - **Zarya’s General Store**, a key source of supplies and trade
  - **Harrison’s Manor**, where relics, curiosities, and old knowledge are gathered and studied
- Myeve also includes armorers, market activity, housing, and other practical support for expedition life.

# 3. History
- Myeve was established as a safe foothold in the Arc Wilds.
- Over time, repeated returns by adventurers helped cement its role as a staging ground, refuge, and place of exchange.
- Its economy has grown through exploration, artifact recovery, supply trade, and the constant movement of fortune-seekers in and out of the wild frontier.
- As more relics and discoveries have passed through Myeve, the town has become increasingly tied to ancient ruins, lost histories, and strange magical systems buried in the Arc Wilds.

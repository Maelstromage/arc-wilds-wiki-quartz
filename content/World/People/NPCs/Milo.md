# Milo

- Small human girl found crying on a mossy stone pillar in the jungle pond within [[D&D/Campaigns/DM/00 West Marches/World/Locations/Bone Speaker Jungle]]
- Wears animal skins
- Has very dark skin
- Speaks a structured but unfamiliar language the party could not immediately identify
- Somehow recognized Papajan's name or something very close to it
- Carried a small bone necklace taken from the ruined tent nearby
- Was brought along by the party toward her village after they rescued her
- Her origins and connection to Papajan remain unclear

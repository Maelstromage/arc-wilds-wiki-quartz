# Location: Mount Celestia

## Rumors
- Mount Celestia is a divine mountain where master builders, holy powers, and celestial beings dwell.
- Some say the greatest crafted works in existence can be found there.

## What is Known
- Mount Celestia is a celestial realm reached by way of the [[The Infinite Staircase]].
- Wilbur guided the party there in search of a master builder.
- It contains grand dwarven works, radiant heights, and paths leading deeper into divine lands.
- The party found a dwarven mountain city there and met the master builder [[Glenn]].

## History
- The party reached Mount Celestia while seeking someone worthy to build their fortress.
- Their journey there began with Wilbur's portal from the swamp and the climb up the Infinite Staircase.
- In Mount Celestia, they gained the lead that sent them onward toward Asgard and the quest for one of Idunn's golden apples.

## People
### [[Wilbur]]
- Unicorn guide who led the party here

### [[Glenn]]
- Celestial master builder willing to build the party's fortress for the right price

## Loose Threads
- What other cities, powers, and craftsmen exist in Mount Celestia?
- Who rules the dwarven works within it?
- What is the full relationship between Mount Celestia and the other divine realms nearby?

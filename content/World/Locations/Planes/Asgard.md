# Location: Asgard

## Rumors
- Asgard is the shining hall-realm of gods, warriors, ravens, and impossible light.
- It is said that fallen warriors feast and fight there forever.
- Some say the Golden Orchard there holds apples of youth and renewal guarded by divine law.

## What is Known
- Asgard is a divine realm reached by the party after passing through [[D&D/Campaigns/00 West Marches/World/Locations/Planes/Mount Celestia]].
- It contains the Bifrost, Odin's hall, vast training grounds, and the presence of gods and valkyries.
- Colors there appear richer and more intense than in the material world.
- The Einherjar train, fight, die, and rise again in its fields.
- [[Odin]] rules there and his ravens Huginn and Muninn watch all who approach.
- The Bifrost is the great rainbow bridge leading into the city of the gods.
- Heim stands as a guardian before Odin's hall and tests those who seek entry.
- The Golden Orchard lies within Asgard and is associated with [[Idunn]] and her apples of youth, renewal, and immortality.

## History
- The party entered Asgard while pursuing the bargain needed to secure Glenn's services as a fortress builder.
- There they learned that [[Idunn]] had been taken by [[D&D/Campaigns/00 West Marches/World/People/NPCs/Loki]] and that Odin would reward her return with one of her golden apples.
- The party crossed the Bifrost, were observed by Huginn and Muninn, spoke with a Valkyrie, passed Heim's test of sacrifice, and entered Odin's hall.

# Idunn

- Goddess associated with youth, renewal, and golden apples
- Missing from Asgard for months
- Said to have been lured away by [[D&D/Campaigns/00 West Marches/World/People/NPCs/Loki]], who tricked her by disguise and deceit
- Her golden apples are precious enough to serve as the price for Glenn's fortress-building services
- Her imprisonment is now central to the party's divine quest

# Wandering Encounter: Wandering Caravan

## What It Is
- A traveling caravan with performers and a scouting faerie.

## What is Known
- A faerie scouts ahead for the caravan.
- The caravan performs for rich people.
- They are known to steal from those rich audiences during performances.
- They do not steal from poor people.

## Behavior
- The caravan uses performance as cover for theft.
- If the scouting faerie is attacked, she offers to grant wishes.
- Those wishes are not true miracles but illusion-based tricks or convincing falsehoods.

## People
### Scouting Faerie
- Scouts ahead for the caravan
- Offers false or illusory wishes if attacked

## Loose Threads
- Who leads the caravan?
- Why do they spare the poor but rob the rich?
- How often do their false wishes lead to trouble later?
- Is the faerie loyal, trapped, or simply amused by the work?

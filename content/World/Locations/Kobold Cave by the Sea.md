# Location: Kobold Cave by the Sea

## Rumors
- By the shore there is a small cave where kobolds gather.
- A dragon lives in the cave and the kobolds worship it.
- The kobolds there also worship Mammon.
- The place was first found by Linla, the mermaid.

## What is Known
- Kobold Cave by the Sea is a small cave on the shore.
- A dragon lives within the cave.
- Kobolds also dwell there.
- The kobolds worship the dragon.
- Linla, the mermaid, was the one who found the area.

## History
- The cave came to notice after Linla found it near the sea.
- It is now known as a place where kobolds and a dragon coexist under worshipful terms.
- The presence of Mammon-worship among the kobolds suggests the site may hold more than simple draconic devotion.

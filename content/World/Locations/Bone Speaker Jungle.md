# Location: Bone Speaker Jungle

## Rumors
- The jungle around Bone Speaker Village is full of strange things that do not belong together. [[2021-05-28]]
- Travelers may find abandoned structures high in the trees. [[2021-05-28]]
- A T-Rex hunts somewhere in the deep jungle. [[2021-05-28]]
- Some say there are paths in the jungle that lead to places of impossible magic and stranger beings. [[2021-05-28]]
- There is said to be a newly built temple somewhere north of the village, with humanoid skeletons hanging around it.

## What is Known
- Bone Speaker Jungle is the greater jungle region surrounding Bone Speaker Village. [[2021-05-28]]
- It contains dangerous wilderness, hidden structures, and strange encounter sites. [[2021-05-28]]
- A treehouse was found there, with a boot below it containing a severed or trapped foot. [[2021-05-28]]
- Inside the treehouse, there was a kettle with water but no tea. [[2021-05-28]]
- A T-Rex was active in the region and had a nesting area with eggs. [[2021-05-28]]
- The jungle can connect or lead into stranger places, including the region around the Veggypygmy Volcano. [[2021-05-28]]
- Maru was gravely injured in the jungle.
- Lizardfolk attacked him and later came back for his body.
- There was a lagoon area associated with a corpse and the corpse of a giant turtle.
- Lizardfolk were seen coming out of the water and tending to one of their own.
- Guta came out yelling for Maru.
- The party later returned to the village and took part in a funeral ceremony.
- Toki Pona was learned there.
- Jax comes from this tribal people.
- Somewhere north of the village, there is said to be a newly built temple with humanoid skeletons hanging around it.
- Taat was saved by the group.
- Taat was missing a leg.
- Face paint associated with this region or its people included two black lines and one dot above the head.
- There is a cave in the region.
- A note or saying tied to the area states: "The Queen of Bermandia lays behind the wall. May her rest be forever peaceful."

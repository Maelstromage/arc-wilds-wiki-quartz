# Loki

- Trickster god tied to Asgard
- Adopted son of [[Odin]]
- Lured [[Idunn]] away through disguise and deception
- Said to be holding Idunn in a cave
- Considered too dangerous to confront directly in straightforward battle
- The party intends to outwit rather than overpower him
- A figure of chaos, cunning, and layered motives

# Issa

- Loyal handmaiden to [[Cylindra Montroma]]
- Drow woman who found Cylindra after her escape into the jungle
- Approached the party cautiously at arrow-point before recognizing Cylindra's allies
- Led or accompanied a retinue of allied Drow soldiers loyal to Cylindra
- Reunited emotionally with Cylindra in the jungle near [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Part of the loyalist side of the internal struggle surrounding Cylindra's house

- Was strongly aware of the smell of dwarves on them [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- Returned to the party after being attacked from stealth [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Unleashed a breath attack that dropped four people [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]
- Was driven back toward his hoard by an illusion of a red dragon [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]

# Martha

- Giantess of [[The Steading]]
- Roughly 150 feet tall
- Warm, delighted by little folk, and eager to host guests
- Preparing a feast for a festival honoring [[Grolantor]]
- Asked the party to deal with pests raiding her food stores
- Her husband had previously killed her tiny helpers after blaming them for bringing rats into the house
- Drove her husband off with a rolling pin after he attacked the party
- Promised that [[Jesse]] would be arriving the following day with a lead toward a trickster god on the next layer

# Location: Zarya's General Store

# 1. Rumors
- “That drow woman runs the shop like she owns the whole town.”
- “She’s got land—prime land. Just waiting for the right moment.”
- “You don’t haggle with her. She already knows what you’ll pay.”
- “Adventurers leave better equipped than they deserve.”
- “She remembers what you buy… and what you forget.”

# 2. What is Known
- Zarya’s General Store is a well-stocked, organized supply shop in Myeve.
- It is a dependable place for adventurers, travelers, and townsfolk to acquire practical goods before heading into the Arc Wilds.
- The atmosphere is warm, efficient, and active, with a steady flow of customers preparing for travel or expedition work.
- The shop regularly carries common adventuring supplies, including:
  - Rations
  - Waterskins
  - Bedrolls
  - Rope
  - Torches
  - Lanterns
  - Oil
  - Chalk
  - Pitons
  - Hammers
  - Sacks
  - Healer’s kits
  - Basic supplies
  - Simple weapons such as daggers, darts, crossbows, and ammunition
- Prices are fair, though not especially generous.
- Inventory may shift over time depending on what local adventurers are doing and what demand has increased.
- Zarya is attentive enough to remember what customers regularly buy, what they neglect to buy, and what that says about them.

# 3. History
- Adventurers in Myeve regularly restock here before expeditions.
- At some point, discussion arose about buying land through or from Zarya.
- Zarya revealed that she owns land of her own.
- She has spoken of plans to build something there once she finds what she considers a strong husband.
- This has established her not only as a shopkeeper, but as someone with longer-term ambitions in Myeve.

# 5. Loose Threads
- What kind of future is Zarya truly trying to build in Myeve?
- Who, in her eyes, qualifies as a strong husband?
- What does she intend to build on the land she owns?
- Will she remain a shopkeeper, or grow into a larger supplier, merchant power, or patron of expeditions?

# Amanda

- Pale woman in black encountered near [[Magic Tower]]
- Wore a black corset and long flowing dress
- Had large unblinking eyes and an unsettling, watchful presence
- Claimed to be good at finding people and escorting them
- Seemed surprised that the party could see her
- Carried a necromantic ankh necklace tied to the guiding of spirits
- Vanished immediately after the ankh was stolen from her during the night
- Appears tied to death, spirits, or the passage of souls
- Her exact nature remains uncertain

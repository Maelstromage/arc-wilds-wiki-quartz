# Location: Green Elf Forest

## Rumors
- A forest of green-clad elves lies somewhere beyond the usual roads. [[2020-12-20]]
- The elves there wear green outfits marked with gold leaves. [[2020-12-20]]
- Trouble once broke out there over a father’s sword. [[2020-12-20]]
- The forest has seen death and restoration both. [[2020-12-20]]

## What is Known
- Green Elf Forest is an elven forest. [[2020-12-20]]
- The forest stands near the [[D&D/Campaigns/00 West Marches/World/Locations/Ancient Elvish City]] and serves as part of its outer protection, helping keep the hidden city from being found.
- There is a small eatery in the forest called Black Castle, owned by hill dwarves and known for serving mini burgers.
- The elves associated with it were described as wearing green outfits with gold leaves. [[2020-12-20]]
- The party went there during the events of the 2020-12-20 session report. [[2020-12-20]]
- Conflict broke out there because Vanessa wanted her father’s sword. [[2020-12-20]]
- The sword was nearly stolen by her father, Faraclaw. [[2020-12-20]]
- Three elves later had to be revived. [[2020-12-20]]
- The party ultimately revived the three elves and was given treasure. [[2020-12-20]]

## History
- During the events of the 2020-12-20 session report, the party traveled to Green Elf Forest. [[2020-12-20]]
- A conflict there centered on Vanessa, her father Faraclaw, and a sword. [[2020-12-20]]
- The elves of the forest are tied to the Ancient Elvish City rather than being wholly separate from it.
- The events led to the deaths or near-deaths of three elves. [[2020-12-20]]
- The party later acquired a blue flower from White Peak Mountain and used it to restore the fallen elves. [[2020-12-20]]
- After the elves were revived, the party received treasure. [[2020-12-20]]

# Jax

- Bartender at Odin's Eye in Myeve
- Native witch doctor from Toki Pona Village
- Has learned Common
- Older notes suggest there was a time when he did not speak Common well or at all
- May speak Bone Speakers
- Quiet
- Observant
- Controlled
- Carries guilt, though he does not show it openly
- Stole a sacred diamond necklace from his homeland
- Was exiled or forced to leave his people because of the theft
- Wants the necklace returned to his village
- Cannot or will not return it himself
- Possesses a stolen diamond necklace carved with a skull and tribal headdress motif
- The necklace is said to hold 8 charges
- The necklace can grant +10 stealth to the whole party for 10 minutes
- Can serve as a quest giver tied to the necklace and his homeland

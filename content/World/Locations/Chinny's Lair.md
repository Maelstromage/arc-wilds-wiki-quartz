# Location: Chinny's Lair

## Rumors
- Somewhere beyond the jungle there is said to be a hidden lair full of gold, books, and strange games.
- Some travelers speak of a horned host who invites adventurers to gamble with their lives for treasure.
- There are stories of contestants walking away rich and others dying in spectacular fashion.

## What is Known
- Chinny's Lair is a lavish hidden lair reached through magic portal travel.
- The party entered it after being invited by [[Chinny]] while resting in [[Bone Speaker Ruins]].
- The lair is built within an underground cavern or cave system but is furnished with surprising luxury.
- It contains treasure, artifacts, paintings, and a vast library of old books.
- Chinny treats the place as both home and stage, using it to host elaborate games for adventurers.
- The lair includes private guest quarters, dining space, and multiple purpose-built challenge rooms.
- Known challenge spaces include a ladder room, a large chamber for illusion contests, a room with concentric glowing rings over a spike pit, a lava room with a tightrope, and a final room with numbered cards.
- Unseen servants operate within the lair, serving food and tending to guests.
- Contestants can win large amounts of gold there, but some of the games are deadly.

## History
- The party first reached Chinny's Lair after Chinny opened a portal for them during a night spent sheltering in Bone Speaker Ruins.
- There the party met other contestants and took part in a sequence of competitive games hosted by Chinny.
- Over the course of those games, contestants were eliminated through portals, dangerous traps, and at least one fatal lava challenge.
- The party survived the competition, left with substantial winnings, and was later returned to the jungle with their original task still unfinished.

## People

- Master of the lair and host of the games

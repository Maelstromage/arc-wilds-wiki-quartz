# Xavriel

- Arrogant white-haired Drow wizard
- Found in the heart of [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Present in the chamber of the ley line and suspended pure life substance
- Uses the temple's inner workings as a life-force refinery rather than merely studying abandoned ruins
- Can transmute the suspended blood there into [[Philosophers Stones]]
- Philosopher’s Stones can grant immortality, heal wounds, or turn objects and limbs into solid gold
- Tied to some of the deepest magical secrets within the ruins
- Knowledgeable about ancient Elvish writing, alchemy, transmutation, and the temple's hidden systems
- Identified the suspended blood as pure life substance that drains life from the surrounding area
- Demonstrated the creation of philosopher's stones from the suspended blood
- Produced stones with protective, healing, and dangerous gold-transmuting effects
- Encased an unstable gold-transmuting stone in glass after it turned a creature's hand to gold
- Asked the party to retrieve an unharmed rogue gelatinous blood clot for his research
- Claimed he was still piecing together the side effects the temple's magic was having on local wildlife, including undead creatures in the canyon
- Agreed to assist with the zombie T-Rex problem in exchange for the rogue specimen
- Gave the party a Necklace of Fireballs to use against the undead T-Rex even after the retrieval failed
- Gave Glint a silver signet ring for future contact and possible work in Zianza'doc
- Clinical, logical, and emotionally detached; tends to treat catastrophe as useful data
- Views others as potentially useful to his research, but not necessarily as equals

# Location: Spirit Cave

## Rumors
- There is a cave where spirits are trapped and cannot leave.
- Those who enter feel the presence of the dead all around them.
- Somewhere inside is the remnant of an old bookshelf that nearly rotted away.
- One surviving book from the cave was sold to John Harrison.

## What is Known
- Spirit Cave is a cave haunted or inhabited by trapped spirits.
- The spirits within it cannot leave.
- At some point, there was a bookshelf inside the cave.
- The shelf had almost entirely rotted away.
- One book survived there.
- That surviving book was later sold to John Harrison.

## History
- Spirit Cave became a place where spirits were trapped and unable to pass on or depart.
- By the time it was examined, most of the shelf and its contents had decayed away.
- One surviving book was recovered and eventually sold to John Harrison.

## People
### John Harrison
- Acquired the surviving book from Spirit Cave

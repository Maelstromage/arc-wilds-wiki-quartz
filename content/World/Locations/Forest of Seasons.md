# Location: Forest of Seasons

## Rumors
- The Forest of Seasons changes as one travels through it.
- Each hour of travel turns the forest into a different season.
- Spring becomes summer, summer becomes fall, and fall becomes winter.
- It is said the veil between the Feywild and the Material Plane is thin there.

## What is Known
- Forest of Seasons is a magical forest.
- Travel through the forest is marked by shifting seasonal changes.
- Each hour of travel changes the season experienced within the forest.
- The cycle is known as spring, summer, fall, and winter.
- The place is rumored to be touched by the Feywild.
- The boundary between the Feywild and the Material Plane is believed to be thin there.

## History
- The forest is known for its unusual cycle of seasons within the span of a single journey.
- Over time, it has gained a reputation as a place where the natural order is unstable or enchanted.
- Its link to the Feywild has become one of its defining legends.

# Wilbur

- Friendly talking unicorn first encountered in a swamp while the party traveled away from Bone Speaker Village
- Cheerful, conversational, and surprisingly casual about impossible things
- Spoke Common and could converse clearly with the party
- Enjoyed honey-oat rations
- Opened a portal to the [[The Infinite Staircase]]
- Guided the party into [[D&D/Campaigns/00 West Marches/World/Locations/Planes/Mount Celestia]] and onward toward divine realms
- Suggested Mount Celestia as the place to find truly masterful builders
- Appeared to be a benevolent celestial guide with unusual freedom of movement between realms
- Fell into the [[D&D/Campaigns/00 West Marches/World/Locations/Planes/River Styx]] during the descent toward Carceri
- Survived being pulled from the Styx, but lost his memory, identity, and portal magic
- Was left as a hollow, confused remnant of himself after the river stripped away what he had been

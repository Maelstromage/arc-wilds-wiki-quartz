# Location: Carceri

## Rumors
- Carceri is a prison plane from which few, if any, escape.
- Some call it Tartarus.
- The ground itself can kill the unwary.
- Those who go there do not return unchanged.
- The very land seems hostile to mortal life.
- Some whisper of a confessional threshold within the plane that strips denial away before deeper passage is allowed.

## What is Known
- Carceri is a prison plane, also known as Tartarus.
- It is like a string of pearls, each orb the size of a material plane, and crossing from one to another carries travelers deeper through its layers.
- As you go lower through each layer, the worlds grow smaller and farther apart.
- It is a place of extreme hostility and imprisonment.
- The environment is unnatural and deadly.
- It is considered nearly impossible to leave once entered.
- The party reached Carceri after passing through the Gray Wastes and the River Styx.
- The layer of [[Cathrys]] is marked by red jungle growth, razor grass, hidden acid pits, and scattered miserable settlements built from sharp or rotting materials.
- Within Carceri there can exist divine pockets that do not match the prison around them, such as [[The Steading]], a green giant-held realm nested inside Cathrys.
- Abi described the next lower layer, [[Minethys]], as a cold sandblasted wasteland where the wind and yellow sand can strip flesh from bone.

## History
- The party arrived in Carceri during the events recorded in the session now known as The Descent into Carceri.
- Their arrival came after a journey through the Gray Wastes, the River Styx, the blackstone dock, Kronos's prison, and the confessional archway deeper within the plane.
- After surviving the first hazards of Cathrys, the party was guided by [[Abi]] through the red jungle toward [[The Steading]].
- There they found one possible lead toward Loki through [[Jesse]], who is said to know a trickster god on the next layer.

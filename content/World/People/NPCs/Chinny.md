# Chinny

- Mysterious horned man encountered by the party while they rested in [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Appeared as a well-dressed man in his mid-twenties with dirty blond hair, a neat beard, bright blue eyes, and two curly yellowish horns
- Wore a pristine black suit despite emerging from the jungle at night
- Carried a staff topped with a bright green emerald
- Charming, theatrical, and cryptic
- Invited the party through a portal into [[Chinny's Lair]] to compete in a series of dangerous games for gold
- Claimed that knowledge, not gold, was his true treasure
- Keeps a lavish hoard of artifacts, books, paintings, and wealth in his lair
- Hosted multiple elimination-style contests involving strength, wit, deception, chance, and nerve
- Allowed players to leave some rounds voluntarily, but made clear that later challenges could become lethal
- Brought in unseen servants, luxurious meals, and carefully staged challenge rooms as part of the experience
- The party suspected he might actually be a brass dragon based on his horns, manner, wealth, and lair
- Returned the party to the jungle after the games with their winnings and the stone tablet still in their possession

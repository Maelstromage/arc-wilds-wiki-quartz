# Location: Veggypygmy Volcano

## Rumors
- A giant volcano rises somewhere beyond the safer paths. [[2021-05-28]]
- Strange little plant-folk live around it and worship what they do not understand. [[2021-05-28]]
- Deep within it lies a chamber of rich tapestries, a great floor pillow, and an urn of terrible heat. [[2021-05-28]]
- Wishes have been granted there before. [[2021-05-28]]

## What is Known
- The location is centered on a giant volcano. [[2021-05-28]]
- Veggypygmies live outside it in a settlement or village-like arrangement. [[2021-05-28]]
- The veggypygmies worshiped the party after the strange magical events that took place there. [[2021-05-28]]
- They wore headdresses and fed the party. [[2021-05-28]]
- The veggypygmies tried to lead the party downward into the volcano toward their god. [[2021-05-28]]
- A tunnel led down into the volcano. [[2021-05-28]]
- Inside was a chamber with colorful tapestries, a giant purple floor pillow with gold trim and tassels, and a brass urn that radiated intense heat. [[2021-05-28]]
- An efreeti emerged from the urn and granted three wishes. [[2021-05-28]]

## History
- During the events of the 2021-05-28 session report, the party was scattered by strange magical effects tied to sigils and teleportation. [[2021-05-28]]
- They emerged near or within the region of the giant volcano. [[2021-05-28]]
- The veggypygmies treated them as figures of significance and brought them upward, then tried to guide them down toward their god. [[2021-05-28]]
- In the chamber below, the party encountered the efreeti in the brass urn. [[2021-05-28]]
- The efreeti granted wishes to the party. [[2021-05-28]]
- After the wishes were made, the veggypygmies vanished with the efreeti. [[2021-05-28]]

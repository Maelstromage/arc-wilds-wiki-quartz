# Region: Black Tooth Marshes

## Rumors

- Black Tooth Marshes is a dangerous swamp region shaped by old powers and things better left alone.
- It was named for the Black Tooth lizardfolk who once lived there.
- Strange things in the marsh drove many of those lizardfolk away.
- A swamp hut and an older temple both lie somewhere within the marshes.

## What is Known

- Black Tooth Marshes is a swamp region.
- The region is named after the Black Tooth lizardfolk.
- Lizardfolk once lived throughout the marshes in greater numbers.
- At least some of them later fled the region.
- The marsh contains both a swamp hut and a place known as the Black Dragon Temple.

## History

- Black Tooth Marshes was once associated closely with the Black Tooth lizardfolk.
- Over time, forces in the marsh caused many of them to leave.
- The region still holds older structures and lingering powers beneath the swamp.

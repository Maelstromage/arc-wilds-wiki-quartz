# Drow Pursuers of Cylindra

- A Drow faction or pursuing force aligned against [[Cylindra Montroma]]
- Active in the jungle around [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Associated with spider imagery and the worship of Lolth
- Involved in a coup or internal Drow power struggle
- According to Cylindra, she was sent away on a scouting mission so she could be isolated and killed
- Continues to hunt Cylindra even after she fled and went to ground
- Uses scouts, ambushes, deception, and direct violence
- Was willing to search roads, jungle routes, and ravines to recover or kill its target
- Includes disciplined armed Drow and at least some lesser followers or acolytes
- Opposed by Drow still loyal to Cylindra, including her handmaiden [[Issa]] and allied soldiers
- The struggle around Cylindra is not simply exile but an active succession dispute within her house
- Operates with persistence and coordination rather than acting like a random wandering group

# Location: Timberfarm Village

## Rumors
- It is said that a group of people chose to build outside the safety of Myeve and paid for it with their lives.
- Their settlement now lies in ruin, and a dragon is said to rule over what remains.
- Kobolds carry treasure into a lone tower at the back of the village for their green dragon master.

## What is Known
- Timberfarm Village was a settlement founded beyond the safety of Myeve.
- The village was overrun and ruined after its people died.
- Twig blights now infest the settlement in great numbers.
- The only relatively safe places in the village are inside the ruined houses.
- A single tower stands toward the back of the village.
- Kobolds are said to bring treasure to that tower for a green dragon.

## History
- At some point, settlers chose to build Timberfarm Village outside the protection of Myeve.
- The settlement ultimately fell, its people dying and its structures collapsing into ruin.
- In the aftermath, the village became overrun with twig blights and fell under the shadow of a green dragon's rule.

# Location: Savage Hammer Dwarves Village

## Rumors
- A dwarf village of the Savage Hammers lies in the wild lands beyond Myeve. [[2020-12-27]]
- The village is one of three dwarf villages in the region. [[2020-12-27]]
- A dragon has been flying closer and closer to the dwarf villages. [[2020-12-27]]
- The folk there still carry old dwarven history with them, whether they know it or not. [[2020-12-27]]

## What is Known
- Savage Hammer Dwarves Village is a dwarf village in the region. [[2020-12-27]]
- It is one of three dwarf villages connected to older dwarven settlement in this land. [[2020-12-27]]
- When the party arrived during the events of the 2020-12-27 session report, there were no dwarves in the village because they had gone to attack orcs. [[2020-12-27]]
- Falstad was taken there after his leg was broken. [[2020-12-27]]
- Falstad offered the party a favor for saving his life. [[2020-12-27]]
- Swiftwing is Falstad’s mount. [[2020-12-27]]
- Keenclaw is Falstad’s wife. [[2020-12-27]]
- Sharpbeak is Falstad’s son. [[2020-12-27]]
- Bagin is the village elder. [[2020-12-27]]
- Tysetysh is a wizard, something described as very unusual for dwarves. [[2020-12-27]]
- The village welcomed and fed the party during later visits. [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- The party met a blacksmith there and the blacksmith's daughter. [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- A dwarf in the village lent the party a bag of holding. [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]

## History
- The village existed before the founding of Myeve. [[2020-12-27]]
- The dwarves of this region were permitted to come into the land with Helm’s blessing. [[2020-12-27]]
- Savage Hammer Dwarves Village is part of a wider dwarven presence in the region tied to older offshoots of dwarven folk. [[2020-12-27]]
- During the events of 2022-02-23 Session 4, the village again served as a place of refuge, trade, food, and social interaction for the party. [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- During the events of 2022-03-02 Session 5, the party returned to find the village destroyed and its people dead. [[2022-03-02 Session 5]]

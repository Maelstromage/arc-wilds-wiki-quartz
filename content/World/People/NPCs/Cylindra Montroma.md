# Cylindra Montroma

- Drow woman of royal blood
- Wears military-style garb adorned with spider motifs
- Was found hiding in an abandoned barn north of Myeve
- Claims she was sent on a scouting mission as part of a coup meant to isolate and kill her
- Cannot safely return home while her enemies remain in power
- Seeks to reclaim her position and survive the coup against her
- Says she wants to lead the Drow away from the worship of Lolth
- Claims she wants to end the cycle of internal Drow infighting
- Papa Jan believed she was telling the truth and was genuinely desperate
- Offered the party positions of power and resources if they helped restore her
- Paid 100 gold up front to have a quest posted on her behalf
- Possesses a family signet ring bearing the symbol of Lolth covered in spider webs
- Was later found alive at the bottom of a ravine after an apparent attempt on her life
- Claimed she had been pushed
- Surrounded by dangerous enemies and uncertain allies
- Has a younger sister attempting to usurp her place through lies and court intrigue
- Believes her mother was misled against her while she was away
- Reunited with her loyal handmaiden [[Issa]] and a retinue of allied Drow soldiers
- Gave the party a silver spider-shaped hair clip bearing her house crest as a token of trust

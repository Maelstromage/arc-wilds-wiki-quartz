# Location: Bone Speaker Ruins

## Rumors
- There is an ancient ruin site somewhere in the jungle tied to a long-lost people. [[2022-02-09 Session 2]]
- A temple crowned with a great golden orb stands there. [[2022-02-09 Session 2]]
- The ruins overlook or surround a vast circular canyon.
- Another temple stands in the center of that canyon.
- Green-scale kobolds have been seen around the site, searching for a way inside.
- Some believe the central temple drains life from the canyon itself.
- There are whispers that the ruins hide an entrance into the Underdark.

## What is Known
- Bone Speaker Ruins is an ancient ruin complex in the jungle. [[2022-02-09 Session 2]]
- The site is tied to an ancient race the explorers hoped to learn about. [[2022-02-09 Session 2]]
- The ruins are defined in part by a great circular canyon.
- Crossing the canyon on foot takes roughly four hours.
- One temple stands on the edge of the canyon.
- That edge temple bears a large golden orb at its top. [[2022-02-09 Session 2]]
- Another temple stands in the center of the canyon.
- A hidden cave in the surrounding jungle can serve as a sheltered resting place near the ruins.
- A steep ravine in the surrounding jungle forms another dangerous feature of the area.
- A winding cave passage leads down into the canyon below.
- The canyon floor is dense, humid jungle inhabited by large dinosaurs and coordinated pack hunters.
- The canyon is permeated by necromantic energy that can cause dead creatures and bodies to rise as undead.
- The canyon walls contain veins of rich silver ore in at least some places.
- The final approach to the temple may be hidden beneath massive overgrowth, with ancient stone steps buried under foliage.
- The temple at the canyon center is an ancient moss-covered pyramid with wide stone steps covered in vines.
- Around halfway up, the stairs include pressure plates set on every other step. [[2022-02-16 Session 3]]
- The entrance includes a door marked with three heads: a drow, a wood elf, and a high elf.
- A blood-filled basin can open the way inward.
- A monkey called [[Longtail]] has lived in part of the temple and can open the inner golden door for food.
- The deeper temple contains mithril machinery chambers, hidden passages, a glass elevator chamber, a living blood ooze, and a ley-line chamber containing suspended pure life substance.
- The central temple functions as a life-force extraction and refinement site, drawing on surrounding vitality for dangerous magical work.
- The site contains an opening that appears to connect toward the Underdark. See [[Underdark Entrances]].
- [[Xavriel]] was found in the sanctum and was using the temple's inner workings to refine Philosopher's Stones.
- The temple is tied to strange undead side effects in the surrounding canyon, including a zombie T-Rex encountered just outside after the party's departure.
- The orrery was later removed and reassembled at John Harrison’s.

## History
- During the events of the 2022-02-09 Session 2 report, the party learned from Jax of an ancient temple in the forest connected to an ancient people. [[2022-02-09 Session 2]]
- After surviving the jungle and the attack of the giant tyrannosaurus zombie, the party fled and eventually arrived in front of a temple with a large golden orb at the top. [[2022-02-09 Session 2]]
- Later clarification established that this temple was part of the larger site now referred to as Bone Speaker Ruins.
- At some later point, three adventurers entered the temple and recovered the golden orrery.
- During the events of 2022-02-16 Session 3, the party used clues from the upper temple to make their way toward the temple in the canyon center. [[2022-02-16 Session 3]]
- There they encountered awakened gorillas, a crocodile pit, secret paths, animated objects, and the burned remains of a lizardfolk shaman trying to resurrect a dragon. [[2022-02-16 Session 3]]
- During the events of 2022-02-23 Session 4, the party continued deeper into the orrery complex, fought revived robots and bound will-o’-wisps, and copied highlighted text revealed by setting the orrery to the present date. [[2022-02-23 Session 4]]
- Their discoveries connected the site to solstice timing, ancient elven lands, and the path to Shu.
- Later exploration revealed more of the temple's inner structure, including vast mithril machinery, elemental pillar mechanisms, a hidden glass elevator room, and a ley-line chamber containing pure life substance.
- The party later negotiated directly with [[Xavriel]], who was conducting dangerous transmutation work in the sanctum using refined life substance from the site.
- During that same expedition, the party learned of a rogue blood ooze specimen, found an apparent entrance toward the Underdark, and left the temple only to be attacked again by a zombie T-Rex in the canyon jungle.
- Further exploration confirmed that the surrounding canyon jungle is itself a major hazard, not merely the ruins at its heart.

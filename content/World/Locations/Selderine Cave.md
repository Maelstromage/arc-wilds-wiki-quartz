# Location: Selderine Cave

## Rumors
- A cave near the water holds ancient elvish drawings on its walls.
- The images show two lovers leading other elves.
- The cave preserves scenes from the Golden Age and the beginnings of magic.
- A strange little wooden box sits at its center.
- Sea hags haunt the waters nearby and claim the river by the cave.

## What is Known
- Selderine Cave is a cave marked by ancient drawings of elves.
- The wall images show two lovers leading a group of other elves.
- The drawings are said to come from the Golden Age and from the beginnings of magic.
- In the center of the cave stands a tiny wooden box covered in ivy.
- The box is painted white and blue.
- The box is a folding boat.
- Sea hags linger around this place.
- The sea hags claim or control the river near the cave.

## History
- Selderine Cave appears to preserve ancient memory or teaching through its wall drawings.
- The imagery ties the site to the Golden Age and to early magical history.
- Over time, the surrounding waters came under the influence of sea hags, who now dominate the river near the cave.

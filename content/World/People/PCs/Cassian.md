Cassian  
Bard  
Half-elf
 
Trained by the notorious Red Palace Opera, Cassian had a long and storied career at the royal houses of [Region]. His life at court consisted of extravagant fêtes, dazzling performances, and the deadliest secrets. And while his patrons waxed poetic about his sumptuous tales, they never saw the dagger hidden in garments, or the poison in the punch bowl. His skill with the pen was matched only by his ability to extract secrets from loose-lipped royals — and sow distrust among the key players of [Region]’s politics. His life of fatal opulence ended, however, when Cassian and his partner, Calver, were betrayed by the very spymaster who trained them. With his partner and only true friend dead, Cassian narrowly escaped with his life. He’s spent the subsequent years hiding in a provincial town, where he makes a living performing at a roadside inn. He all but forgot about his past, until one day he received a letter from someone claiming to be Calver.

# Location: Pluton

## Rumors
- Pluton is a city of judgment where souls and petitioners wait to be sorted into the afterlife.
- Those who stand upon its shores watch passing ferries with the stillness of the already judged.

## What is Known
- Pluton lies along the River Styx in the Gray Wastes.
- It is associated with judgment and the waiting dead.
- The party passed it while traveling by ferry toward Carceri.
- Silent figures watched the ferry from its shore, including forms Papa Jan found strangely familiar.

## History
- During the ferry journey on the River Styx, the party passed Pluton and witnessed the silent watchers there.

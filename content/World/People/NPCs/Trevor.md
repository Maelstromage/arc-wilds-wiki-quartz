# Trevor

- Triceratops encountered in the canyon around [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Hungry and separated from her family in the canyon below
- Befriended the party after Corneleus spoke with her and fed her
- Ate a great deal of the party's rations
- Revealed that a Tyrannosaurus Rex had blocked the passage back to her home and killed one of her companions
- Fought bravely alongside the party against the Tyrannosaurus Rex
- Gored the predator in the ribs and knocked it to the ground
- Later found her companions dead and eaten
- Departed alone to seek vengeance against the sharp-toothed predator

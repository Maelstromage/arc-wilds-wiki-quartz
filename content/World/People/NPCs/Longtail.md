# Longtail

- Monkey living within [[Bone Speaker Ruins]]
- Lives near the temple's inner golden door
- Opens the golden door for visitors in exchange for food
- Has been doing this for a couple of years
- Knows that an orrery once stood within the temple but was removed
- Knows that holes in the ceiling once contained snakes, though only dead ones remain now
- Was encountered again by the party when they left the temple through the golden doors
- Small, opportunistic, and accustomed to bargaining for snacks
